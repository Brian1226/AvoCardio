AvoCardio

Our Story

Nice to meet you all! The four of us are students from San Jose State University (SJSU) who eagerly formed a team before the first day of the "Senior Design Project" course. During the initial stages, we decided to create some kind of web application because we all had experience with web development, whether from previous classes or internships. The next step is brainstorming ideas for the app. There were so many ideas being pitched and discarded until finally, we thought, "Why not create an all-in-one solution to simplify the path to a healthier lifestyle?" Senior design projects must address and solve some kind of issue in society, so our idea was perfect. And thus, AvoCardio was born.

Our drive to develop a solution that is not only user-friendly but also impactful for society has been a powerful source of motivation for us. As we journeyed through creating AvoCardio, we applied our skills, delved into new technologies, and overcame numerous challenges along the way; lots of bugs and procrastinations :D. The image on the right was taken on December 8, 2023, where we showcased our project in an expo. We had lots of fun!
